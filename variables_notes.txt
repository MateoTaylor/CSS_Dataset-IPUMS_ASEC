Variables pulled from CPS:

Personal - Core - Demographics
RELATE, Relationship to household head
AGE, Age
SEX, Sex
RACE, Race
MARST, Marital Status
POPSTAT, Adult civilian, armed forces, or child	

Personal - Core - Work
EMPSTAT - Employment Status
LABFORCE - Labor Force Status
OCC - Occupation
OCC2010 - Occupation 2010 basis

Personal - Core - Education
EDUC - Educational attainment recode

Personal - Core - Family Interrelationships
FAMUNIT - Family unit membership
FAMREL - Relationship to family 

Personal - Core - Technical
FAMID - Census Family ID

Personal - ASEC - Income -
FTOTVAL - Total family income (census version)
INCTOT - Total personal income
INCCHILD - Income from child support
CTCCRD - Child Tax Credit

Personal - ASEC - Work - 
OCCLY, Occupation last year
OCC10LY, Occupation last year 2010 basis
CLASSWLY, Class of worker last year
WORKLY, Worked last year
INDLY, Industry last year

Personal - CSS - Child Support Supplement
CSSUPPAID, Amount of child support paid last year (only 20-23)
CSOUT, Child has parent that lives outside the household
CSWHY, Reason child does not have a biological/adoptive parent who lives outside the home
CSLEGAL, Ever had a legal child support agreement
CSAGREE, Ever had a child support agreement other than legal
CSTYPE, Type of agreement (recode)
CSPAY, legal agreement included payments
CSYEAR, Year first had agreement or made agreement legal
CSSUPP, Other parent was supposed to pay child support last year
CSREAS, Reason other parent was not supposed to send payments last year
CSDDUCT, Any payments were supposed to be deducted from other parent's paycheck
CSWAYPAY, Method of child support payment delivery
CSCHG, Legal payment amount changed
CSCHGYR, Year payment amount changed
CSFREQ1, Frequency of payments - no change or before change
CSBACK1, Child support included back payments - no change or before change
CSFREQ2, Frequency of payments - after change
CSBACK2, Child support included back payments - after change
CSRECV, Actually received any child support last year
CSALL, Actually received all support payments supposed to receive
CSTIME, How many payments were received on time
CSFULL, How many payments received were the full amount
CSAMDUE, Amount of child support due
CSAMREC, Amount of child support actually received
CSCONTACT, Had any contact with other parent last year
CSVIS, Other parent has visitation privileges
CSPHYCUS, Has physical custody agreement with other parent
CSLEGCUS, Has legal custody agreement with other parent
CSDAYS, Number of days other parent spent with child last year (doesn't exist for 2023)
CSYRDIV,Year of most recent divorce or separation
CSYRMAR, Year of marriage from most recent divorce or separation
CSWORK, Working at the time of most recent divorce or separation
CSWORK5, Worked at some point 5 years prior to most recent divorce or separation
CSELIG, Eligibility to answer Child Support Supplement
CSSUPPWT, Child Support supplement weight	
CSSUPPWT, Child Support supplement weight
